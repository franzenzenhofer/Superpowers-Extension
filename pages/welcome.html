Why Is It Cool?

  • Cross-domain fetches: Dare to fetch from anywhere. No more same-origin headaches!
  • AI Chat & Tools: Use Superpowers.OpenAI to interact with GPT or generate images.
  • Credentials Manager: Securely store OAuth keys and tokens (e.g., for Google Analytics, GSC).
  • Environment Variables: Quickly set and retrieve env vars in the browser.
  • In-Page Scripting: Your page now has super hooks for debugging, console intercepts, and more!

Getting Started:
1) Open the Side Panel or the Credentials Manager to configure API keys and tokens.
2) Insert <meta name="superpowers" content="enabled" /> in your page.
3) Now your page can call window.Superpowers methods directly (e.g. Superpowers.fetch).
